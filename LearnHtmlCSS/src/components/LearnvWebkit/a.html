Webkit 线性渐变示例

探索 CSS 渐变的无限可能

基础双色渐变

background: -webkit-linear-gradient(45deg, #ff6b6b, #4ecdc4);

三色渐变

background: -webkit-linear-gradient(90deg, #667eea, #764ba2, #4ecdc4);

多色彩虹渐变

background: -webkit-linear-gradient(135deg, #ff6b6b 0%, #ffa726 25%, #ffee58 50%, #4ecdc4 75%, #667eea 100%);

多重渐变叠加

background: -webkit-linear-gradient(45deg, rgba(255,107,107,0.6), rgba(78,205,196,0.6)),
-webkit-linear-gradient(135deg, rgba(102,126,234,0.6), rgba(118,75,162,0.6));

动画渐变

background: -webkit-linear-gradient(45deg, #ff6b6b, #4ecdc4, #45b7d1, #667eea, #ff6b6b);
background-size: 400% 400%;
animation: gradientShift 8s ease infinite;

图案渐变

background: -webkit-linear-gradient(90deg, transparent 0%, transparent 49%, rgba(255,255,255,0.3) 49%, rgba(255,255,255,0.3) 51%, transparent 51%, transparent 100%),
-webkit-linear-gradient(45deg, #667eea, #764ba2);
